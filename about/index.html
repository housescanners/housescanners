About Our Company

House Scanners is full service, family owned and operated home inspection company that provides professional home inspections and an array of environmental testing services for nearly a decade throughout Wake, Chatham, Durham, Orange, Alamance, & Lee counties.

Our home inspectors are trained professionals, licensed by the North Carolina Home Inspection Licensure Board, are fully insured, and engaged in continuing education to keep up with the most current industry trends and construction methods. We take pride in performing every home inspection in accordance with the North Carolina Home Inspector Licensure Board's Standards of Practice & Code of Ethics.

Our office has worked with local and national realtor offices such as Allen Tate Realtors, Carolina Trace Properties, Chatham Homes Realty, Coldwell Banker, Keller Williams, My Dog Tess, Re/MAX, Straight Up Realty, Total Source Realty, and many more.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide our clients with the highest level of professionalism and moral integrity when performing our home inspection and environmental analysis services. We strive to exceed the needs and expectations of our clients and exemplify the best that the home inspection industry has to offer by consistently delivering courteous & dependable services at competitive prices.

Customer service and satisfaction are our #1 priorities!
